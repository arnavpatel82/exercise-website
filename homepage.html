THE IMPORTANCE OF PHYSICAL WELLBEING

Physical health is the ability to maintain a healthy quality of life that allows you to make the most of your daily life without excessive fatigue or physical stress.
This involves taking care of your body and acknowledging that your daily habits and behaviors have a significant impact on your overall health, well-being and quality of life.
